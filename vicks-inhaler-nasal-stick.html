OVERVIEW

Vicks Inhaler is a nasal decongestant stick (0.5 ml medication) it contains the active ingredients: Menthol (125mg), Camphor (50mg), Siberian Pine Needle Oil (10mg). It also contains methyl salicylate.

When you inhale these actives through your nose they work on the lining of your nasal passages to ease breathing and help relieve a stuffy nose caused by colds or allergic reactions.

PACKAGE INFORMATION

DIRECTIONS

For Nasal Inhalation
Adults and children over 6 years: Vicks Inhaler is used by inserting the inhaler into each nostril, holding the other nostril closed and inhaling deeply. It can be used as frequently as needed.

WARNINGS

Do not use Vicks Inhaler: if you are allergic to any of its ingredients or after the expiry date.
Not recommended for children under 6 years.
If your symptoms persist, worsen or if you notice any unexpected side effects, consult your doctor or pharmacist.
Do not store above 25°C
Keep out of the sight and reach of children
